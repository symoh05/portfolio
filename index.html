Who am I?

I'm Simon Ngugi, a passionate Full Stack Web Developer with a Bachelor's degree in Information Technology from KCA University. I specialize in building modern, responsive websites and mobile applications using technologies like PHP, Python, JavaScript (React, Vue), Java, C, HTML, CSS, Tailwind CSS, and more.

I'm based in Kiambu, Kenya, and I'm always excited to collaborate with clients who value innovation and quality in digital products.
